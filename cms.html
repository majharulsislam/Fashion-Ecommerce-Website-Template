Standard Delivery*

Pending availability and credit card verification, orders will arrive within 3-5 business days.

Shipments to Alaska, Hawaii, US Territories and APO/FPO addresses take additional time.

Standard Delivery*

Pending availability and credit card verification, orders placed before 1 pm (ET) will arrive within 2-3 business days

Not available for items shipping to PO Boxes, U.S. Territories and APO/FPO addresses.

Not available for all items on site. See product details for any shipping restrictions.

Next Business Day*

Pending availability and credit card verification, orders placed before 1 pm (ET) will arrive within 1 business day.

Not available for items shipping to PO Boxes, U.S. Territories and APO/FPO addresses.

Not available for all items on site. See product details for any shipping restrictions.

Manhattan Same-Day Delivery

Same-day delivery service in Manhattan: $25 or free with ShopRunner

Orders placed by 1 pm (ET) Monday-Saturday & by 11 am (ET) on Sunday will be delivered the same day, pending availability and credit card verification; orders placed after the cutoff time will be delivered the next day

Same-day delivery hours are 6-9 pm (ET) Monday-Saturday & 4-7 pm (ET) on Sunday

Available for select items in the New York store and for customers in most areas of Manhattan; delivery service is not available for zip codes 10032, 10033, 10034 & 10040

ShopRunner 2 Day FREE*

ShopRunner offer FREE 2 day shipping and returns

Pending availability and credit card verification, orders placed before 1 pm (ET) will arrive within 2-3 business days.

Not available for items shipping to PO Boxes, U.S. Territories and APO/FPO addresses.

Not available for all items on site. See product details for any shipping restrictions.

Gift Card Shipping

Currently Gift Cards can only be purchased or redeemed when shipping to US addresses.

Buy Online, Pick Up in Store

In store pickup service is available at select Saks stores

Orders must be placed by 4pm local time for pick up same day

When your order is ready, you'll receive an email confirmation that it can be picked up in store. Please note, pick-up times can be longer due to increased demand over Thanksgiving and Christmas.

Photo ID is required for store pickup.

Sales Tax

Sales tax will be charged for purchases shipped to all states in the US, except Alaska, Delaware, Montana, New Hampshire, and Oregon.

No sales tax is charged on the purchase of a Gift Card.
